/puhr-SIHM-muhn/

the edible fruit of a number of species of
trees in the genus Diospyros.

  • specifics
  • nutrition
  • culinary
  • sources

The word Diospyros comes from the ancient Greek
words “dios” and “pyron.” A popular etymology
construed this as “divine fruit.”
